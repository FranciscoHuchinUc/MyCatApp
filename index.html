What you need to know

Proper nutrition can help ensure that your cat has optimal health, disease resistance, a healthy coat, and energy. These factors can lead to fewer behavioral problems, a good quality of life, and a long life.

Cats require high quality fat and protein in their diet. They cannot be vegetarians because their bodies need essential nutrients, such as taurine, from meat. Dog foods and human foods do not contain enough of these nutrients for cats, so cats should not be fed a dog or human diet.

Although your cat needs certain amounts of each specific nutrient to be healthy, more is not always better. This is particularly true of vitamins and minerals, so the use of supplements is usually not necessary if you are feeding a balanced and complete diet.
